# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis & Forecasting  
*(Superstore Dataset Project)*

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to uncover key business insights and forecast future sales trends. Using the Sample Superstore Dataset, I performed data analysis and built an interactive dashboard in Power BI to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales and profit trends across regions, categories, and segments  
- Identify top-performing and loss-making areas  
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights  
- Perform time-series forecasting to predict future sales  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source  
- **Data Analysis Techniques** – Aggregation, filtering, trend analysis  
- **Forecasting** – Time-series trend analysis  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains retail sales information including:
- Order Date  
- Region  
- Category & Sub-Category  
- Sales & Profit  
- Customer Segment  
- Ship Mode  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Metrics
- Total Sales: **492.65K**  
- Total Orders: **2.27K+**  

---

### 🔹 Visual Insights
- Sales distribution by Category and Sub-Category  
- Profit and performance by Region  
- Sales by Customer Segment  
- Sales by Shipping Mode  
- Monthly and yearly sales trends  

---

## 📈 Sales Forecasting
- Performed time-series analysis on historical sales data  
- Identified trends and seasonal patterns  
- Forecasted future sales to assist in planning and decision-making  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Technology** category contributes the highest share of total sales  
- Certain sub-categories show high sales but low profit, indicating cost inefficiencies  
- Sales show seasonal spikes, especially towards year-end  
- Standard Class shipping mode dominates overall sales  
- A few regions contribute significantly higher profits than others  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Helps identify high-performing product categories  
- Detects loss-making areas for cost optimization  
- Supports data-driven decision-making  
- Assists in future sales planning through forecasting
